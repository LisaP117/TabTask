Directions

Set your maps to Manchester airport and exit the M56 and get in lane for Terminal 1 (T1). At the set of traffic lights, go straight ahead for T1.

At the roundabout, take the 5th exit and follow signs for T1 Meet & Greet (T1 M&G). After 0.1 miles take the first avialable left turn and continue 0.1 mile to a roundabout. Take the first exit and entrance to T1 M&G is then immediately on your right.
